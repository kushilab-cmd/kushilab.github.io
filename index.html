About Me

I am a healthcare executive with over 35 years of leadership experience spanning the Indian Navy and private healthcare. My expertise includes healthcare operations, hospital administration, clinical governance, healthcare transformation, disaster medicine, digital health implementation, and medical education.

Throughout my career, I have led large multidisciplinary healthcare teams, commissioned major healthcare infrastructure, implemented enterprise digital health systems, and directed strategic initiatives that improved quality, efficiency, and patient outcomes. I remain committed to advancing healthcare through innovation, leadership, and lifelong learning.
